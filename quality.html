IATF 16949 Standard

IATF 16949 is the global automotive industry standard for quality management systems, established by the International Automotive Task Force (IATF).

It is based on the ISO 9001 quality management standard and provides additional automotive-specific requirements.

Key Quality Requirements

  • Consistently provide products meeting customer, statutory and regulatory requirements
  • Enhance customer satisfaction through effective application of the quality management system
  • Implement processes for continual improvement of the quality management system
  • Define the overall context, stakeholders, and their expectations
  • Clearly state quality objectives and identify new business opportunities
  • Put customers first and ensure their needs are consistently met
  • Identify and address risks associated with the organization
  • Work more efficiently to increase productivity and reduce internal costs
  • Implement and document corporate responsibility policies

Continuous Improvement

By meeting the requirements of the IATF 16949 standard, automotive organizations can demonstrate their commitment to quality, safety, and continuous improvement throughout the supply chain.
